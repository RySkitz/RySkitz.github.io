(ABM) Accountancy, Business and Management

About the ABM Strand

The ABM strand offered by the Santo Rosario Academy of Alupay Rosario Batangas, Inc introduces the concepts of accounting, business management, corporate operations, and financial management. Building a strong foundation for those who will take business and management programs.

Once you choose this strand, you can lead a successful career as an Entrepreneur, Advertising Professional, Marketing or Sales Representative, Accountant, Account Manager, Bank Teller, Restaurateur, Corporate Manager, Retail Manager, Financial Adviser, or Economist.

(STEM) Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics

About the STEM Strand

An inquiry and research-based program, the STEM strand in Senior High School offered by Santo Rosario Academy of Alupay Rosario Batangas, Inc. It exposes pupils to harder math and science ideas and tries to lay the groundwork for their future college degrees

You can pursue a variety of careers under the track, including those of a pilot, architect, astrophysicist, biologist, chemist, engineer, dentist, nutritionist, nurse, doctor, and many others.

(HUMMS) Humanities and Social Sciences

(HUMMS) Humanities and Social Sciences

About the HUMMS Strand

The HUMMS strand offered by the Santo Rosario Academy of Alupay Rosario Batangas, Inc is intended for those who are curious about the world beyond. In other words, you are ready to face the world and engage in meaningful conversation with others.

If you choose this path, you might eventually choose to work as a teacher, psychologist, lawyer, author, social worker, reporter, or even a priest. The goal of this strand is to help you communicate more effectively.
